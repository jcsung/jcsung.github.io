About

Hi, my name is Jeff Sung, and I am a Front End Web Developer. My primary focus is React/Redux development, but I am also proficient in other frameworks and libraries including jQuery, Bootstrap, Metal.js, and AlloyUI.

I also have extensive experience developing for and using Liferay Portal CE and EE (6.1, 6.2, and DXP). At Liferay, working on agile software development teams, I developed Metal.js components for internal tools, created AlloyUI components for internal and client-facing administrative products, and implemented custom themes for internal and client-facing applications. I also integrated new front end components with the back end, conforming to existing development patterns.

Outside of web development, I enjoy playing board games (Brass, Food Chain Magnate, and Cosmic Encounter being my favorites), photography, watching and discussing TV and movies (Sci-Fi, heists, and bad movies especially), eating, and taking pictures of my food.

Academia

In a previous life, I worked towards becoming a computational chemist in academia. I have an MS in Chemical Physics from University of Minnesota - Twin Cities an and MS in Chemistry from University of California, San Diego.

I specialized in molecular mechanics simulations of chemical systems, having done academic research using Monte Carlo simulations, Molecular Dynamics simulations, and Brownian Dynamics simulations, to develop highly accurate empirical small hydrocarbon force fields, to analyze the behavior metal-organic framework and zeolitic materials, and to investigate the binding kinetics of influenza neuraminidases with various ligands, respectively, in the labs of Professors J. Ilja Siepmann, Francesco Paesani, and J. Andrew McCammon, respectively. My presentation and publication records are available on request.

While I have permanently transitioned out of that industry, presently I continue to apply the 9 years of development experience, data science skills, analytical experience, presentation skills, teaching experience, debugging skills, and problem-solving expertise that I gained to projects in both personal and professional contexts.
